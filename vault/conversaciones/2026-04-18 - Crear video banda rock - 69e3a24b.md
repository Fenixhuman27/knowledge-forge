### 🎬 PROMPT – CAL (VOCALISTA) – FLOW

Copiá y pegá esto tal cual:

```
Cinematic rock performance video, same man as reference image, maintain exact facial features, hairstyle, beard, outfit (red plaid shirt open, Nirvana t-shirt, jeans), same identity consistency across frames.

Scene: small live rock club stage, realistic environment, light haze and smoke, warm amber and soft blue lighting, backlights creating depth, visible amps and drum kit behind.

The man (Cal) is the lead singer, standing center stage, holding a microphone very close to his mouth, singing intimately, almost whispering into it. His expression is intense, slightly conflicted, emotionally drawn, subtle tension in his eyes.

Camera: slow cinematic push-in, slight handheld movement for realism, shallow depth of field, focus on face and microphone, soft background blur.

Motion: minimal but natural — breathing, slight head tilt, small mouth movement, subtle body sway, fingers adjusting grip on microphone.

Mood: dark, sensual, emotional tension, like being addicted to someone you shouldn't want.

Lighting: warm highlights on face, soft shadows, cinematic contrast, no overexposure.

Style: ultra realistic, film look, 35mm cinematic, high detail skin texture, natural imperfections, no plastic look.

IMPORTANT:
- Do not change face or identity
- Do not exaggerate expressions
- No fast movements
- No lip-sync exaggeration
- Keep realism and continuity between frames
```

### 🔧 CLAVES DE ESTE PROMPT (para que entiendas por qué funciona)

Esto no es relleno, es lo que te va a hacer ganar:

- **“micro muy cerca de la boca”** → te salva el problema de sincronización con la canción
- **movimiento mínimo** → evita glitches de IA
- **“identity consistency” + outfit específico** → evita que muten los personajes
- **“slow push-in + handheld leve”** → da vida sin romper estabilidad
- **emocionalidad controlada** → no sobreactúa (error típico de IA)

### 🎬 PROMPT – CAL (LIP-SYNC CONTROLADO – 8s)

Copiá y pegá esto:

```
Continue from the same man and same scene, exact identity consistency, same face, same outfit, same microphone.

The singer (Cal) is performing live on a small rock club stage, holding the microphone extremely close to his mouth, partially covering his lips at all times to reduce visible lip-sync mismatch.

He is clearly singing this exact phrase in sequence, with natural pacing across 8 seconds:

"Velvet shadows on the floor
Candlelight behind your eyes
Every time you close that door
I forget what's wrong or right"

Timing and delivery:
- Slow, intimate vocal delivery
- Slight pauses between lines
- Mouth movement subtle and partially obscured by microphone
- No exaggerated articulation

Camera:
- Locked close-up shot at the beginning (first 3 seconds)
- Then very slow push-in + slight handheld movement
- Keep framing tight on face and microphone

Motion:
- Minimal head movement
- Slight forward lean into the mic on each line
- Eyes mostly closed at first, slowly opening by the third line
- Small emotional tension in eyebrows and jaw

Lighting:
- Warm amber light on face
- Soft blue and red stage lights behind
- Slight flickering to simulate live environment

Background:
- Blurred band members playing softly
- No focus shift away from singer

Mood:
- Intimate, hypnotic, emotionally conflicted
- Like he's trapped in the moment

IMPORTANT:
- Keep microphone covering part of the mouth at all times
- Do not show wide open mouth movements
- No fast cuts
- No identity change
- Maintain realism and continuity
```

---

### 🧠 Esto es lo que te arregla el problema

- **Micro tapando parcialmente la boca** → clave, disimula desincronización  
- **“slow delivery + slight pauses”** → estira la frase a 8s sin que se note raro  
- **movimiento mínimo** → evita que la IA “improvise” labios  
- **ojos cerrados al inicio** → reduce foco en boca en los primeros segundos  

---

Si querés hacerlo aún más pro (nivel clip real), el siguiente paso sería:
- dividir la frase en **2 prompts de 4 segundos** y unirlos → mejora mucho la ilusión de sincronización

Si te interesa, te armo esa versión también, que queda todavía más creíble.
