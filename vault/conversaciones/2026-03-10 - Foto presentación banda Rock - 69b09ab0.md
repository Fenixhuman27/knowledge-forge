### Asistente

Te dejo un prompt pensado para que **Gémini genere algo cinematográfico y realista**:

Member 2 (Grunge Rock): slightly rebellious look with textured hair and light stubble, casual layered clothing like flannel or vintage band tee with jacket, relaxed posture, intense but thoughtful expression. Authentic grunge vibe but not messy.

Member 3 (Modern Metal): strong presence, well groomed beard or clean shave, long or medium hair but neatly styled, wearing dark stylish clothes or a tailored leather jacket, elegant yet powerful metal aesthetic.

Member 4 (Dark / Gothic Rock): pale elegant aesthetic, dark hair, subtle gothic fashion like black coat or modern gothic jacket, mysterious expression, refined and artistic rather than extreme.

All four musicians look very attractive and charismatic in their own style. They stand close together like a real band, subtle attitude differences showing their musical identity.

Lighting: dramatic cinematic lighting, soft shadows, studio quality.  
Background: dark concert stage or atmospheric studio with subtle smoke and colored lights.  
Mood: confident, artistic, modern rock band introduction photo.  
Style: hyperrealistic photography, 50mm lens, shallow depth of field, high detail, magazine-quality band promo.
:::

Un pequeño consejo: si Gémini te da algo medio genérico, puedes agregar al final:

- **“vertical poster composition”** (si quieres estilo póster)  
- **“album cover style”** (si quieres que parezca portada de disco)  
- **“band standing in a slight V formation”** (queda muy bien para bandas).

Si quieres, también puedo hacer **una segunda versión del prompt todavía más potente**, con estética tipo **portada de disco de los 90 / 2000**, que suele funcionar mejor para rock. 🎸
