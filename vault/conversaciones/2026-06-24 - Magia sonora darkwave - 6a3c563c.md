Alternative rock, early 2000s influence, 150 BPM, major key. Bass and drums aggressive and forward in the mix. Electric guitars are dominant, slightly overdriven, rhythmic and raw. Minimal pop polish, no stadium elements, no gang vocals, no chants, no crowd sounds. Studio band energy with live performance feel. Lead vocal slightly gritty, confident, youthful but not pop-clean. Strong groove focus, guitar-driven arrangement, tight rhythmic structure, emotional but energetic tone. Includes one melodic electric guitar solo section. Radio-ready alternative rock, closer to garage rock energy than pop rock sheen.
